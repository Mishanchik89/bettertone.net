Types of hearing diagnostics

We test your hearing with modern precision techniques
Tonal threshold audiometry
An effective method shows the degree of hearing loss and the perception of sounds at different frequencies. Received audiogram is used for the choosing and adjustment of hearing aids.
Impedansometry (tympanometry)
Examination of middle ear functionality. Shows the condition of the eardrum, auditory tube and other structures. Examines acoustic reflexes.
Otoacoustic emissions (UAE)
A method for diagnosing auditory receptors. A hearing examination shows how the hair cells of the inner ear work. Reveals sensorineural hearing loss.

How do We examine Your Hearing

We consult and examine
The doctor asks you, carefully examines the ear and eardrum with a special device. This allows you to see inflammation and sulfur plugs.
We carry out speech audiometry
The doctor speaks phrases in different loudness at different distances. The test shows how you hear normal, loud speech and whispering, whether you have a hearing impairment.
We do audiometry
The specialist carries out a thorough diagnostics using high-precision equipment. On the basis of the results we determine the type and degree of hearing loss and choose the method of hearing correction.

Why is it important to check the hearing of an infant / child in time ?

Every year in Ukraine, about 1-2% of children are born hearing impaired. If the right moment is missed, the child will not speak and will not be able to go to ordinary school. Early screening of hearing in newborns allows to correct impairments in early levels. Thanks to this, the child develops along with his peers. Caring for children's hearing is in the hands of adults!
